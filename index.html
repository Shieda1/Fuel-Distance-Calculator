What is the fuel distance calculator?
The fuel distance calculator is a tool that measures the cost and the fuel distance if the quantity and efficiency of the fuel are known. How to calculate fuel distance?
To calculate fuel distance you should use the following formula:
distance = fuel efficiency * fuel quantity
